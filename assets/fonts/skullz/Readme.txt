Enclosed in this archive are:    SkullZ (MAC T1 font) 
                                                  SkullZ.bmap 

For all you poison, pirate and skull freaks, here is a collection 
of 52 skulls in one postscript type 1 font...

System 7+ users just drag the icons over your System folder...

Use capitol letters A to Z and lower case letters a to z to access the characters.

SkullZ font was compiled over a long period of time, through the
gruesome process of collecting skulls from all over the world! 

SkullZ font is freeware... Give it to your friends and enemies.
